### Legend

Black and bold font are mandatory cBioportal headers

* **PATIENT\_ID** = "submitter\_donor\_id"
* **START\_DATE** = SURGERY\_DATE - DATE\_OF\_DIAGNOSIS = DAYS (IN INTEGER)
* **STOP\_DATE** = BLANK
* **EVENT\_TYPE** = 'SURGERY'
* SAMPLE\_ID = "submitter\_specimen\_id"
